Yosemite Notification Center
Widget Requests Center

One of the great Yosemite's features definitely is the Today view in Notification Center. Now it supports widgets, which is very useful. Unfortunately, Apple doesn't provide us with a tool that we can use to request widgets to the developers. So here it is. Now you can post here the widgets that you want and developers can see what you want.
Scroll to the very bottom to see how you post one yourself.

How to Submit Your Ideas

This website doesn't support posting with browser yet. To submit a widget idea, please send an email to yosemite.widget@gmail.com.

Your subject will be the title of your post, and your content will be the content of the post. You can also put your username on the last line (optional).
